7-Day Cut (Fat Loss) Plan

Achieve your fat loss goals with this tailored workout and meal plan designed to burn fat while maintaining muscle mass.

Workout Plan

Day 1: Full Body Strength Training

Focus on compound movements to maintain muscle while burning fat.

4 Sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise

  • Squats
  • Deadlifts
  • Bench Press
  • Pull-ups
  • Overhead Press

Day 2: HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

30 minutes of intense exercises alternating between 30 seconds of work and 30 seconds of rest.

Exercises: Burpees, Jump Squats, Mountain Climbers

Day 3: Active Recovery (Light Cardio & Stretching)

30 minutes of light walking or cycling and 20 minutes of stretching or yoga.

Day 4: Upper Body Strength (Push)

Push heavy weights for the upper body, focusing on chest, shoulders, and triceps.

4 Sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise

  • Incline Bench Press
  • Overhead Press
  • Chest Flys
  • Lateral Raises
  • Tricep Dips

Day 5: Pull Day (Back & Biceps)

Focus on pulling movements for back thickness and bicep strength.

4 Sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise

  • Deadlifts
  • Pull-ups
  • Barbell Rows
  • Bicep Curls
  • Face Pulls

Day 6: Leg Day (Hamstrings, Quads & Glutes)

Focusing on strengthening the full lower body, especially hamstrings and glutes.

4 Sets of 10-12 reps for each exercise

  • Leg Press
  • Romanian Deadlifts
  • Lunges
  • Calf Raises

Day 7: Rest & Active Recovery

Another recovery day with stretching, yoga, or a light walk to reduce soreness.

Home Workout Alternatives

Day 1: Full Body Circuit

Perform 3 rounds of:

  • Bodyweight Squats: 20 reps
  • Push-ups: 15 reps
  • Lunges: 10 reps per leg
  • Plank: 30 seconds

Day 2: Cardio Blast

Perform HIIT at home:

  • Jumping Jacks: 40 seconds
  • High Knees: 40 seconds
  • Mountain Climbers: 40 seconds
  • Rest: 20 seconds

    • Repeat for 4 rounds

Day 3: Active Recovery

Light body movements:

  • 10-minute light walk
  • 10-minute stretching routine focusing on hamstrings, back, and shoulders
  • Yoga: Downward Dog, Child's Pose

Day 4: Upper Body Strength

Home-based upper body exercises:

  • Push-ups: 15-20 reps
  • Chair Dips: 12 reps
  • Plank Shoulder Taps: 20 reps
  • Repeat for 3 rounds

Day 5: Back and Core

Bodyweight-focused back and core workout:

  • Superman Holds: 30 seconds
  • Bird-Dogs: 10 reps per side
  • Side Planks: 30 seconds per side
  • Repeat for 3 rounds

Day 6: Lower Body Burn

Bodyweight exercises for legs:

  • Bodyweight Squats: 20 reps
  • Step-Ups (use a sturdy surface): 10 reps per leg
  • Calf Raises: 15 reps
  • Repeat for 3 rounds

Day 7: Stretch & Relax

Perform a stretching routine for full-body recovery:

  • Cat-Cow Pose: 10 reps
  • Seated Forward Bend: Hold for 30 seconds
  • Butterfly Stretch: Hold for 30 seconds
  • Child's Pose: Hold for 30 seconds
