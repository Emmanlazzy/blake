7-Day Maintenance Plan

Maintain muscle mass with this tailored workout and meal plan. Designed to keep you strong, fit, and healthy without significant gains or fat loss.

Workout Plan

Day 1: Full Body Strength Training

  • Squats
  • Deadlifts
  • Bench Press
  • Pull-ups
  • Overhead Press

4 Sets of 8-10 reps for each exercise, focus on moderate to heavy weight.

Day 2: Cardio + Core Work

20-30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio (e.g., cycling, jogging) followed by 15 minutes of core exercises (Planks, Leg Raises, Russian Twists).

Day 3: Upper Body Strength (Push)

  • Incline Bench Press
  • Overhead Press
  • Chest Flys
  • Lateral Raises
  • Tricep Dips

4 Sets of 8-10 reps for each exercise.

Day 4: Active Recovery (Stretching, Yoga, or Light Cardio)

30 minutes of light stretching or yoga to improve flexibility and recover.

Day 5: Pull Day (Back & Biceps)

  • Deadlifts
  • Pull-ups
  • Barbell Rows
  • Bicep Curls
  • Face Pulls

4 Sets of 8-10 reps for each exercise.

Day 6: Lower Body Strength (Legs & Glutes)

  • Leg Press
  • Romanian Deadlifts
  • Lunges
  • Calf Raises

4 Sets of 8-10 reps for each exercise.

Day 7: Rest & Active Recovery

Take a complete rest day or go for a light walk to help your body recover.

Home Workout Alternatives

Day 1: Full Body Strength Training

Bodyweight Exercises:

  • Bodyweight Squats: 4 sets of 20 reps
  • Push-ups: 4 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Plank Rows (with water bottles): 3 sets of 12 reps per side
  • Glute Bridges: 4 sets of 15 reps

Day 2: Cardio + Core

Cardio and Core Alternatives:

  • High Knees: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Mountain Climbers: 4 sets of 30 seconds
  • Bicycle Crunches: 4 sets of 15 reps per side
  • Plank Shoulder Taps: 3 sets of 20 reps

Day 3: Upper Body Strength (Push)

Upper Body Home Workouts:

  • Incline Push-ups (use a chair): 4 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Tricep Dips (on a sturdy surface): 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Arm Circles: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Wall Push-ups: 3 sets of 15 reps

Day 4: Active Recovery

Light Activities:

  • Yoga Flow (Downward Dog, Cobra, Child's Pose): 15-20 minutes
  • Dynamic Stretching: 10 minutes
  • Light Walk or Marching in Place: 20-30 minutes

Day 5: Pull Day (Back & Biceps)

Pull Exercises:

  • Superman Holds: 4 sets of 15 seconds
  • Reverse Snow Angels: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Doorframe Rows: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Bicep Curls (with water bottles): 3 sets of 15 reps

Day 6: Lower Body Strength

Lower Body Home Workouts:

  • Walking Lunges: 4 sets of 15 reps per leg
  • Wall Sits: 4 sets of 30 seconds
  • Donkey Kicks: 4 sets of 12 reps per side
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets of 20 reps

Day 7: Rest & Active Recovery

Active Recovery Options:

  • Light Yoga Stretch: 15-20 minutes
  • Foam Rolling (if available): 10 minutes
  • Deep Breathing and Meditation: 10 minutes
  • Short Walk or Leisurely Cycling: 20 minutes
