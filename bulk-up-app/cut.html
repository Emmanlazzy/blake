7-Day Cut (Fat Loss) Plan

Achieve your fat loss goals with this tailored workout and meal plan designed to burn fat while maintaining muscle mass.

Workout Plan

Day 1: Full Body Strength Training

  • Squats
  • Deadlifts
  • Bench Press
  • Pull-ups
  • Overhead Press

Day 2: HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

30 minutes of intense exercises alternating between 30 seconds of work and 30 seconds of rest.

  • Burpees
  • Jump Squats
  • Mountain Climbers

Day 3: Active Recovery (Light Cardio & Stretching)

30 minutes of light walking or cycling and 20 minutes of stretching or yoga.

Day 4: Upper Body Strength (Push)

  • Incline Bench Press
  • Overhead Press
  • Chest Flys
  • Lateral Raises
  • Tricep Dips

Day 5: Pull Day (Back & Biceps)

  • Deadlifts
  • Pull-ups
  • Barbell Rows
  • Bicep Curls
  • Face Pulls

Day 6: Leg Day (Hamstrings, Quads & Glutes)

  • Leg Press
  • Romanian Deadlifts
  • Lunges
  • Calf Raises

Day 7: Rest & Active Recovery

Another recovery day with stretching, yoga, or a light walk to reduce soreness.

Home Workout Alternatives

Day 1: Full Body Strength Training

  • Bodyweight Squats: 4 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Push-ups: 4 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Chair Dips: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Plank Rows: 3 sets of 10 reps per side

Day 2: HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

Perform each exercise for 40 seconds, rest for 20 seconds. Repeat the circuit 4 times.

  • High Knees
  • Jumping Jacks
  • Mountain Climbers
  • Squat Jumps

Day 3: Active Recovery (Light Cardio & Stretching)

30 minutes of light activity and full-body stretches.

  • Dynamic stretches (Arm Circles, Leg Swings)
  • Yoga poses (Child's Pose, Downward Dog)
  • Walking or light jogging

Day 4: Upper Body Strength (Push)

  • Incline Push-ups: 4 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Wall Handstand Holds: 3 sets of 20-30 seconds
  • Tricep Dips (using a chair): 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Lateral Arm Raises (with water bottles): 4 sets of 15 reps

Day 5: Pull Day (Back & Biceps)

  • Reverse Snow Angels: 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Superman Hold: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Back Extensions (on the floor): 4 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Bicep Curls (using bags): 4 sets of 15 reps

Day 6: Leg Day (Hamstrings, Quads & Glutes)

  • Step-ups (using a sturdy chair): 4 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Glute Bridges: 4 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squats: 4 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
  • Wall Sits: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds

Day 7: Rest & Active Recovery

Take a light walk, perform foam rolling, or enjoy a relaxing yoga session.

  • Foam Rolling (if available)
  • Yoga poses for relaxation
  • 30 minutes of easy walking or cycling
